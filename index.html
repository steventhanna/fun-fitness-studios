About Us

Welcome to F.U.N. Fitness Studios, LTD . We are a cross circuit training gym, specializing in functional strength training. If you’re looking for a fresh approach to your fitness goals and an ever changing variety of exercises to keep you interested, then look no further!

Fun Fitness Studios is dedicated to creating a unique approach to fitness, combining forgotten tools & modern practices. The year is 2014 are you still exercising like its

1960? Most people are, if you’re in the gym banging out sets on a bench, or if today is legs day, then in the immortal words of celebrity comedian Jeff Foxworthy, “You might be a Neanderthal-neck.”

Long gone are the days of chest, back, & arms! Today, unlike the mid 60s, we know that muscle isolation is great for bodybuilding, but doesn’t do much for core strength. Even if you’re skeptical about change, consider the time you spend with your current workout. How much time do you dedicate to cardio, & weights? Before the days of microwaves and twitter accounts things were simpler, slower. Today, there’s barely enough time to accomplish daily task despite our modern conveniences, and if you’re reading this page, you face the same challenges.

Now do something about it. FUN FITNESS workouts are designed to consolidate your time and maximize your effort! Are you looking to lose weight or gain muscle? The only way to do either or both is diet and exercise! Dieting alone though won’t do much for you overall health other than to strip you of much needed nutrition. Our goal is to help you achieve optimal fitness without injury.

Trainer

F.U.N. Fitness Studios, LTD. is a personal training company, owned and operated by Marcus Brent, a certified fitness professional specializing in optimizing functional strength for everyday needs that help improve quality of life.

Since 1997 Marcus Brent has coached & instructed physical fitness to all ages & fitness levels.

Marcus is a former Chicagoland all around gymnastics champion 1979. In the early 1980s he played football in the Marine Corps, he also trained recruits at the San Diego Marine Corps Depot. Through the years he has continued to maintain his physical training by playing numerous sports along with Cycling, Running, Scuba Diving & inline skating, Most recently at age 44 he competed in Bench America’s “Chicago’s Fittest Man challenge” finishing with the best time {under 2 hours} and placed in the top 5 with no age category.

Marcus Brent is certified by N.P.T.I. National Personal Training Institute and has over 20 years experience in fitness and weight management.
